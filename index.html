Atlas of Public Urbino

“Master students of ISIA made in a one week workshop under my guidance an atlas of the public spaces of Urbino. All the information collected by the students is translated into a graphic information system. Indexes and the chosen graphic language allow the reader to make links, allow him to get out of this book his own story, his own rendition of what public space is nowadays in an Italian city like Urbino”. – Joost Grootens

  • 2013
  • Isia Urbino
  • 2nd Year Collective Book
  • Under the guidance of Joost Grootens

Io questo sogno lo vorrei raccontare

In this project I played with some automated-photography tricks to capture my nights, my movements. To document myself during my dreams I took a light controllerand an intervalometer with long exposures. The project presents eight series of the most significant shots captured during the year, matched with all the relative data of that particular night session.

  • 2012
  • Isia Urbino
  • Under the guidance of Simone Casetta

CSAC 1971–1980

For this project we were asked to make a research upon an italian archive, visiting it and telling a story about it. Our work focused on the photographic exhibitions between 1971 and 1980 at CSAC archive, underlining the most important aspect of the exposed work: its dimension. The book is split in two parts: one showing the photos of the exhibitions in their original size through close-ups, another presenting the same photos as they were presented in the archive folders, with all their classification data.

  • 2013
  • Isia Urbino
  • with Giorgio Fanecco
  • Under the guidance of Leonardo Sonnoli

It's not always easy to get in

During the huge last year snow storm in Urbino I documented the hard conditions in which people was forced to live in. After a photographic reportage I build a website showing my pictures, where everyone was able to upload their own photos of the impressive storm.

  • 2012
  • Self initiated

Pesaro Urbino crafts' map

Concept of a map showing the connections between publishing industries within the province of Pesaro e Urbino. The map shows every location of each single production site, together with roads, transportations and traveling time. In opening the leaflet it is possible to see the morphology of the territory and the comparison of traveling times.

  • 2011
  • Isia Urbino
  • with C.Checcanin, V.Maccari
  • Under the guidance of Luciano Perondi
