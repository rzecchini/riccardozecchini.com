Sansavenir

This book illustrates the critical situation of Ilva Taranto, a notable italian steel plant. The project is built around pictures shot by Pierangelo Laterza, manipulated and distorted to represent the actual conditions of the city. This images are matched with some utopian documents from the Sixties and with contemporary data about the steel plant production and the pollution it provoked.

  • 2013
  • Isia Urbino
  • with A.Busi, F.Conti Picamus, R.Cramarossa, G.Fanecco, T.Zennaro
  • Under the guidance of Mauro Bubbico

Prototype for the Satispay mobile-payment App. Satispay is a service that allows to easily transfer money with a mobile device between people or in customer-seller transactions. The App gives you the possibility to send money and to track your expenses with ease. Soon available.

Atlas of Public Urbino

An atlas of the public spaces of Urbino made up from different projects. I curated the overall design and architecture, focusing on the data gained from the contributors. Indexes and chapters are introduced by a visual representation of the relationships between the members of each group and the urban space in which they live in.

  • 2013
  • Isia Urbino
  • 2nd Year Collective Book
  • Under the guidance of Joost Grootens

Io questo sogno lo vorrei raccontare

I want to tell this dream of mine: an automated reportage to capture my nights and my movements. Made with a light controller and an intervalometer with long exposures, the project presents eight series of the most significant shots captured during a year, matched with all the relative data of that particular night session. Look at the entire series.

  • 2012
  • Isia Urbino
  • Under the guidance of Simone Casetta

CSAC 1971â€“1980

A book about the history of CSAC Archive, Italy. This work is focused upon the photographic exhibitions between 1971 and 1980 and it underlines the most important aspect of the exposed work: its dimensions. The book is split into two parts, the first one showing the photos of the exhibitions in their original size through close-ups, another one presenting the same photos as they were presented in the archive folders, with all their classification data.

  • 2013
  • Isia Urbino
  • with Giorgio Fanecco
  • Under the guidance of Leonardo Sonnoli

It's not always easy to get in

A documentation of the hard conditions in which people was forced to live in during the 2012 huge snow storm in Urbino. A photographic reportage and a website showing my pictures, where everyone was able to upload its own photos of the impressive storm. Visit the website.

  • 2012
  • Self initiated

Pesaro e Urbino map

Concept of a map showing the connections between publishing industries within the province of Pesaro e Urbino. The map shows every location of each single production site, together with roads, transportations and traveling time. In opening the leaflet it is possible to see the morphology of the territory and the comparison between traveling times.

  • 2011
  • Isia Urbino
  • with C.Checcanin, V.Maccari
  • Under the guidance of Luciano Perondi
